Drawing Zentangle Patterns

Drawing zentagle is stress relieving as well as it is fun. You don't need any major experience for zentangling, just some inspiration and creativity.

  1. Get you supplies. You will need paper, penicils, sharpies(think and thin), ruler, Micron Pen(Optional)
  2. Pick what you're going to zentangle. It can be a shape or a picture that is left uncolored. Make sure whatever you pick, you have space to draw you different petterns you don't want something in which you space is limited. In this example I am going to be zentagling the letter A.
  3. First draw you shape and outline it with a sharpie or a dark colored pen.
  4. Next start at either the top, bottom or side of your shape, don't start in the middle because it will harder for you to fill in the rest of it. Start with drawing something like cirlces and lines and coloring in a few here and there. If you want some more zentangle patterns or ideas go to tanglepatterns.com But remember zentangle is about being creative and it is something to do if you are trying to relax, so don't worry about trying to make your patterns perfect or anything.
  5. Continue filling your shape in with different patterns and ideas. Try doing one patern at the top and then another pattern at the bottom or at the side so the differnt patterns don't intermix. If you start seeing the some of your patterns are running into each other, try boxing them of with bubbles or sectioning them off. If it makes it easier divide your shape into sections so you can fill each secction with a different pattern.
  6. Congrats! your finally finished with your zentangle art. If you realize that your shapes or patterns are really big and empty try filling them in with differnt colors. And make sure you don't forget to outline your patterns with sharpies.
